Batch stagger delay
seconds

Pause between a key's own batches, to stay under its per-minute token limit. With one key, total search wait ≈ 3 × this value (since 4 batches stagger sequentially). 3s is a reasonable starting point on the free tier; raise it if you're still seeing rate-limit failures.
